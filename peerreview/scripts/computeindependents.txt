Compute Independents
Automatically determines who is an independent reviewer and who is not. The results are saved, so any changes you have made will be clobbered by running this script.



<h2>Parameters</h2>
<table width="100%">
<tr><td>&#8209;&#8209;minscore [int]</td><td>The minimum score needed to be
good (Default 2)</td></tr>
<tr><td>&#8209;&#8209;numconsecutive [int]</td><td>The number of consecutive
good reviews to be independent (Default 2)</td></tr>
</table>
